Administration Role Description

What to expect as a Studio Administrator

Responsibilities:

  • Oversee daily operations of the studio
  • Manage schedules, payroll, and administrative tasks
  • Coordinate between models, monitors, and technical staff
  • Handle financial records and reporting
  • Ensure compliance with legal regulations
  • Implement and maintain studio policies
  • Assist with recruitment and training

Requirements:

  • 18+ years of age
  • Strong organizational and management skills
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Proficiency with administrative software and tools
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and professionalism
  • Problem-solving and conflict resolution skills
  • Previous administrative experience required

What We Offer:

  • Competitive salary with performance bonuses
  • Career advancement opportunities
  • Professional work environment
  • Comprehensive training
  • Team building activities
