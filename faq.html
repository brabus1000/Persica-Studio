Application Requirements

What are the requirements to apply as a model?

Applicants must be of legal age and possess a valid EU identification card. Non-EU applicants must provide a valid passport. Additionally, you must reside in one of the cities where we operate.

Which cities are currently covered?

At this time, we operate exclusively in:

  • Bucharest
What is the minimum age to apply?

You must be at least 18 years old to apply. We strictly do not employ minors under any circumstances.

Are there specific beauty standards or physical requirements?

No. At Persica Studio, we value diversity and individuality. Physical appearance is not a determining factor in our selection process.

Do you accept transgender individuals, men, or couples?

No. We exclusively work with female models. Our facilities, training programs, and mentoring processes are tailored specifically for women. Unfortunately, we do not accommodate men, transgender individuals, or couples at this time. We encourage those who do not meet these criteria to seek studios that better align with their professional goals.

Is there an application fee? Do I have to pay for a portfolio?

No. The application process is entirely free, and no costs are associated with creating a portfolio or promotional materials.
